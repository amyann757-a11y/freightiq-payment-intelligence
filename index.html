The deeper problem: Euler Hermes and Allianz Trade make billion-dollar coverage decisions using generic financial data — company filings, broad credit scores. They have no access to actual freight payment behaviour. Good clients get rejected. Bad ones slip through. The data feeding their models is wrong, and nobody is fixing it at source.
The solution

Three steps. Zero bad debt.

FreightIQ sits between broker CRMs and insurance underwriters as the data sanitisation and intelligence layer.

🔌

Brokers connect

Plug your TMS or CRM into FreightIQ. Raw payment data is ingested automatically — no manual exports, no formatting required.

Why now

The window is open

Four conditions converging at once — the timing has never been better to build this.

🌍 Geopolitics reshaping trade

New corridors opening between India, SE Asia, Middle East, and the Americas. Brokers onboarding unfamiliar shippers daily — with zero payment history to check against.

📉 Broker margins collapsed

Post-2022 freight recession destroyed margins. Brokers can no longer absorb bad debt. The pain is acute enough that they will pay to solve it.

🔍 Insurer data is broken

Euler Hermes rejects good clients because their models run on inaccurate, generic data. The freight-specific behavioural layer doesn't exist yet — anywhere.

🏦 No one owns this layer

Allianz and Euler cover losses after. Nobody prevents them before. The payment intelligence infrastructure for freight is wide open. No incumbent owns it.
