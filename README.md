FreightIQ is a product design concept for the data intelligence 
layer the freight industry is missing.

Freight brokers extend credit to shippers on faith. When shippers 
don't pay, brokers absorb the loss. Credit insurers like Euler Hermes 
and Allianz Trade exist to help  but they make decisions on generic 
financial data, not actual freight payment behaviour. Good clients 
get rejected. Bad ones slip through.

FreightIQ sits between broker CRMs and insurance underwriters. 
Raw, messy payment data goes in. Normalised, validated, 
freight-specific risk signals come out.

Think Bloomberg for freight payment intelligence  a shared data 
network that gets smarter with every broker who joins.

This repo contains the full product concept: problem framing, 
solution design, and 6 Figma screens covering the landing page, 
analytics dashboard, shipper risk feed, live sanitisation pipeline, 
shipper detail view, and mobile.

Built as a PM portfolio project by someone who has worked inside 
freight brokerage and lived this problem firsthand.
