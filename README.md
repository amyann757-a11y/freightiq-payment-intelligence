# FreightIQ — Freight Payment Intelligence

> The data sanitisation layer the freight industry never had.

![FreightIQ Dashboard](screens/dashboard.png)

---

## The problem

Freight brokers extend credit to shippers on faith. When shippers 
don't pay, brokers absorb the loss.

Credit insurers like **Euler Hermes** and **Allianz Trade** exist 
to help  but they make decisions on generic financial data, not 
actual freight payment behaviour. Good clients get rejected. 
Bad ones slip through.

Nobody is solving the data problem at the root.

---

## The solution

**FreightIQ** sits between broker CRMs and insurance underwriters.

| Input | Process | Output |
|-------|---------|--------|
| Raw messy CRM data | Normalise, validate, deduplicate | Clean verified risk signals |
| Broker payment history | Score shipper behaviour | Risk tier: Green / Watch / Flag |
| Multiple broker sources | Aggregate anonymously | Industry-wide credit intelligence |

---

## How it works

**Step 01 — Brokers connect**
Plug your TMS or CRM into FreightIQ. Raw payment data ingested automatically.

**Step 02 — Data gets sanitised**
Normalised, deduplicated, validated. Messy CRM notes become structured signals.

**Step 03 — Insurers receive clean data**
Euler, Allianz and others receive verified freight-specific intelligence — not generic credit scores.

## Why this gap exists

- Euler and Allianz pull from generic credit bureaus (D&B, company filings)
- Brokers self-report client data with incentive to oversell
- No freight-specific behavioural payment data exists anywhere
- Result: inaccurate scores, rejected good clients, bad ones approved

**FreightIQ fixes the data at source — before it reaches the insurer.**

---

## Market context

- Global freight brokerage market: $70B+
- Post-2022 freight recession collapsed broker margins
- Global trade routes restructuring (India, SE Asia, Middle East corridors opening)
- Brokers onboarding unfamiliar shippers daily with zero payment history
